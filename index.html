Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients: Flour, milk, eggs, sugar, baking powder, butter.
Perfect for breakfast!

Procedure:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and eggs, whisk smoothly.
  3. Heat pan and add butter.
  4. Pour batter and cook both sides.
  5. Serve with syrup.

Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients: Lettuce, tomato, cucumber, carrot, olive oil, lemon juice.
A healthy and refreshing dish.

Procedure:

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Place them in a bowl.
  3. Add olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Toss well.
  5. Serve fresh.

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients: Rice, mixed vegetables, spices, onion, yogurt.
Note: Use basmati rice for best results.

Procedure:

  1. Cook rice until 70% done.
  2. Prepare spiced vegetable mixture.
  3. Layer rice and vegetables.
  4. Cook on low heat (dum) for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.

Grilled Sandwich

Ingredients: Bread slices, butter, cheese, vegetables, chutney.
Quick and tasty snack.

Procedure:

  1. Spread butter and chutney on bread.
  2. Add vegetables and cheese.
  3. Cover with another slice.
  4. Grill until golden brown.
  5. Cut and serve hot.
